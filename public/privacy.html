Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Rep Roulette collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS app or website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal details such as your name, email address, or phone number. The only information the app saves is the groups you create (the group name and the member names you enter). This information is stored locally on your device using the browser/app's local storage and is kept there until you delete it within the app or clear your device's app/browser data.

We do not transmit your groups to our servers, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs to collect additional information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and post the updated policy on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
